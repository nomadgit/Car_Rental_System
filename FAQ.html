What is the minimum age requirements for renting a car?




Renters should be at least 21 years of age with a valid driver's license.

Are there any special discounts available?



Yes, renters who are part of veteran services and those who are booking for marriages, corporate needs get discounts.

Can I transfer my rental to some one else?



No, it is not possible to transfer the rental. Only cancellation is possible

What kind of documents need to presented while pick up?



You will asked to provide a valid driver license, a photo ID.

Do I need to pay deposit to rent a vehicle?



No, it is not needed to pay a deposit for advanced booking. For a rental, a full amount is expected to be paid.

Can I rent a car with my international driving license?



Yes, it is possible, but you will asked to provide passport, a photo ID and a international permit issued by your home country.

How can I cancel my reservation?



You will be able to cancel the reservation in an easy one step process. Once you logged in to your account, you can select the cancel registration option from the reservation tab. There you will be able to cancel a reservation based on the unique reservation ID provided to you while you reserved a car.
