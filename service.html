Rent @ car Services:

Rent @ car is an online car rental company which provides you the service of booking the car without the need of visiting the rental center right from anywhere. Below are the few services we provide for you.

Rent or Reserve a car

The customer will be able to rent a car he likes/wants for a specific period of time. You can also reserve a car The customer can reserve a car for future or the current day. The maximum number of days the car can be blocked will be 2 days. After which, if the customer haven’t rented the reserved car or cancelled it, it will get cancelled automatically.

Cancel Reservation

We give the customers the option to cancel the registration. One can simply login to their account and cancel a particular registration just by checking in the cancellation tab. Since they have their unique registration ID, they will be able to cancel it easily

Personal Driver

We provide our customers with the choice to opt for a personal driver. The option to choose for whether they need a driver service or not can be done at the time of the registration itself.

Special Offers

Rent @ car service provides special support and services for customers who would like to book vehicles for their corporate meetings, functions, marriages, airport travels etc. We provide our customers a great deal of discounted prices.
